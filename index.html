Visit Hyderabad

charminar

Charminar is a symbol of Hyderabad and officially incorporated in the emblem of Telangana

Laad Bazaar

This market has been in operation since the time of the Qutb Shahis and the Nizams. It is close to landmarks such as Charminar, Makkah Masjid and Chowmahalla Palace.

Makkah masjid

The Mecca Masjid is considered to be one of the best architectural works of the Qutb Shahis.[9] It is constructed entirely of dressed stone, rather than rubble or plaster.

Saalar Jung Museum

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa, on the southern bank of the Musi River in the city of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the notable National Museums of India.[1] Originally a private art collection of the Salar Jung family, it was endowed to the nation after the death of Salar Jung III. It was inaugurated on 16 December 1951.
